--- layout: default title: Scheduled maintenance, Sept 26th 2011 ---

ProsePoint Express will be temporarily offline on September 26th 2011 from 02:00am to 06:00am (Greenwich Mean Time) for routine scheduled maintenance. During this time, the ProsePoint Express service and all ProsePoint Express sites will be unavailable.

Whilst we expect the maintenance to take about an hour, we are allocating four hours in case of unexpected difficulties.

We will be logging the progress of this event on the status page at http://status.prosepoint.net.

You do not need to take any action. Your ProsePoint Express site will remain the same before and after the maintenance window.

Thank you.

(Apologies for the short notice and any inconvenience caused. We are running into capacity constraints and need to add more capacity to ProsePoint Express.)
